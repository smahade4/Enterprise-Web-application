About Us

We at Italian Bistro offer eclectic Italian cuisine. Banquette seating and gentle lighting contribute to an intimate and contemporary feel.

Our food is prepared from top quality seasonal ingredients. We also provide "Seasonal Special" dishes which are the seasonal favourites usually. We craft all of our stocks, sauces, breads, pastries from scratch on a daily basis.

Our approach towards the operation of the restaurant is truly co-operative. We value our customers and strive to provide delicious food with best quality. We do offer discounts and promotions to our valued customers for online orders on a timely basis.

Tired of waiting in queue for tables? Do not worry!! There is a special provision for all the customers to reserve a table in advance through our website. Reserve your table in advance for the time and day of your choice and just visit our restaurant at the reserved time.

Not satisfied with our services? Feedback is always welcome and we do pay attention to the comments and suggestions of our customers. Please use the link provided at our page bottom to give your valuable suggestions and comments.
